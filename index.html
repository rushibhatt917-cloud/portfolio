DigiStock — Inventory Management App
🏆 Odoo Hackathon 2026 · Team Lead & Developer
Hackathon · Full-Stack

A modular, real-time Inventory Management System for clothing stores — replacing paper registers and Excel sheets with a centralized web app. Features a live dashboard, receipt & delivery management, internal stock transfers, adjustment logging, and a complete audit ledger across 120+ products in 8 store locations.

Full Stock Ledger — every movement permanently logged for audit trails
